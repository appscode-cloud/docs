# Host AppsCode Platform as Your Own

Welcome to AppsCode Platform's Self-Hosted deployment! Whether you're looking for a quick trial in "Self Hosted Demo" mode or gearing up for a production-ready environment (`Self Hosted Production`), you're in control.


Navigate to [AppsCode Self-Hosted](https://selfhost.appscode.com/appscode). Here you will find your previously generated self-hosted installers.

Click on the `Create New Installer` button to get started. You can either choose deployment type Demo or Production. Provide the required data and click `Done` button to generate the installer. Upon generation of the installer, you will get the documentation how to host AppsCode Server on your own.

To get detailed documentation on `Self Hosted Demo` installer, head over [here](install/selfhosted-demo.md).
